SMX aims to democratize the investment process. It can also be dubbed as the "Chemistry.com" for Investors and Investees. SMX's Syndicate facilitates startup financing with accredited investors on the platform.

How it works

1 Verification & Approval

Set up your account and create a company profile. We'll call you to assess your fundraising goals.

2 Match

Our smart algorithm delivers probable matches based on your capital requirements and company details. Speak with investors who express an interest in your opportunity.

3 Network

Connect with specialist financial service providers to support your transaction, if required, and speed up the deal process.

4 Fundraise

Secure investment and grow your business.
